Angela Axworthy

Currently post-doctoral research fellow at the Max-Planck-Institut für Wissenschaftsgeschichte of Berlin and associate member of the Centre d’Études Supérieures de la Renaissance (Tours, France), I have been working since 2002 on Renaissance epistemology of mathematics. My current research deals with the status of mathematics in the thought of sixteenth-century French mathematicians, notably Oronce Fine and Jacques Peletier du Mans, with the ontological and epistemological status of motion in geometry in the context of the Renaissance Euclidean tradition, as well as with sixteenth-century cosmology.
